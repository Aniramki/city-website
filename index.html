Kharkiv

Founded in 1654, it is home to over 1.4 million Ukrainians and is one of the largest cities in Ukraine. Majestic, bright, and picturesque, it is referred to as the first capital and considered the scientific center of Ukraine. The largest square in Europe is located in Kharkiv and is called Freedom Square. The first airport in Ukraine was built in Kharkiv, and Kharkiv beauties have won the title of "Miss Ukraine" three times. The city is rightfully regarded as the student capital of Ukraine, and Kharkiv National University is one of the oldest higher education institutions in Ukraine.
